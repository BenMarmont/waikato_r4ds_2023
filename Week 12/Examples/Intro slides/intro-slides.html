Pedagogy

Inspired by the carpentries

  • Active learning (practice) is key, this course will have minimal components where I speak to you i.e.lecture. Instead, we will utilise ‘live coding’.
    • Live coding will show you my workflow, my mistakes (everyone makes them) and how I troubleshoot them!
    • When you run into difficulty utilise the chat in class, communicating with others is key to understanding
    • Spaced retrieval
    • Managed cognitive load
